- AutoRaptor is being used daily by the sales team for lead management and follow-ups

- Automated follow-ups over time (days → weeks → months)
- Inventory-aware responses (no incorrect pricing or availability)
- Role-based access (sales vs management visibility)
- Simple reporting/dashboard (lead volume, outcomes, activity)

### Proposed direction (high level)

Replace AutoRaptor with a lean, custom CRM-style system built around:

- A simple sales-facing interface
- AI-driven SMS + follow-ups running in the background
- Reliable inventory syncing
- Focus on replacing actual daily workflows, not rebuilding unnecessary CRM bloat

### Future scope (not in v1)

- Ad management
- Social media growth / content
