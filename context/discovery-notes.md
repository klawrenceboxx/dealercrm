# Discovery Call Notes — AutoRaptor CRM
**Date:** 2026-02-02
**With:** Sleiman Moujaes (Eli's nephew, day-to-day contact)
**Duration:** ~18 min

---

## Pain Points with AutoRaptor

1. **AI feels robotic** — responses are too templated, not human enough
2. **Inventory doesn't sync** — feed pulls from D2C platform but goes stale (showed entries "one month ago" on screen share)
3. **AI gives wrong pricing** — because inventory is outdated, AI tells clients incorrect prices (his words: "tells a client the car is for a dollar when it's for 50 cents")
4. **No role-based access** — wants manager view with cost/mechanic info that salesmen can't see
5. **Cost** — ~$300–400 USD/month for a system they're not fully happy with

---

## Current AutoRaptor Setup

- Leads come in via **ADF email** (not manual entry)
- Inventory synced from **D2C** (external platform) — not updating reliably
- AI sales assistant exists but is **disabled** — they don't trust it
- Pre-built follow-up sequences: texts leads every few days, re-engages cold leads after 3 months
- Round-robin lead assignment across 4 salesmen
- Salesman accounts have limited access vs. admin (Sleiman)
- Walk-in leads: can scan driver's license to auto-fill info
- Dashboard shows: leads in, e-leads, lost, delivered, closing %, emails sent

---

## What They Want in the New System

### Must-Have
- **AI lead qualification** — not to sell cars, but to qualify leads before a salesman has to. Book appointments, schedule meetings, gather info (budget, urgency, trade-in, etc.)
- **Hot/warm/cold classification** — AI scores leads based on response signals
- **AI lead summary** — auto-generated summary per lead stored in CRM
- **Automated follow-up sequences** — replicate AutoRaptor's cadence (every few days, re-engage cold at 3 months)
- **Real-time inventory sync** — accurate vehicle data so AI never gives wrong prices
- **Role-based access** — manager sees cost/mechanic notes; salesmen do not

### Nice-to-Have
- **Contract/offer sheets with VIN lookup** — enter VIN, vehicle info auto-fills; salesman adds client details
- **Carfax integration** — attach Carfax report to a lead's trade-in
- **Social media growth** — Instagram, Facebook, TikTok content (30–40s talking videos). Secondary priority, not part of CRM build
