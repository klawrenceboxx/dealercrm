## Primary Goal
Replace AutoRaptor with a custom-built CRM system.

## Features Discussed

- **Custom Automation** — n8n workflows for lead intake, SMS, follow-ups
- **Lead Capture and Normalization** — capture via ADF email, normalize and import into the new platform
- **AI-Driven SMS Messaging** — qualify leads, personalized follow-ups, schedule calls, hand off to human
- **Automated Follow-up Sequences** — texts at defined intervals (e.g., every 2 days, or after 3 months of no contact)
- **Inventory Integration** — connect to dealership inventory feed; auto-populate car details by VIN
- **Role-Based Access Control** — sales staff vs. management; sensitive financials (repair costs) restricted to managers
- **Dashboard and Reporting** — lead sources, closing percentages, email activity
- **Contract and Document Management** — generate offer sheets and contracts within the system
